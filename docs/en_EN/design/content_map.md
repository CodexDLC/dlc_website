# 🗺️ Content Strategy & SEO

## 1. Localization Strategy
The website serves two primary audiences with distinct content needs.

*   **DE (Primary Market):** German content focusing on local market specifics, subsidies (KfW, BAFA), legal compliance, and regional trust (TÜV/VDE certificates).
*   **EN (Secondary Market):** English content focusing on corporate identity, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) goals, and information for international investors or large-scale EPC partners.

## 2. Technical SEO
To dominate local search results for commercial and residential solar installations.

*   **Schema.org Markup:** Implementation of `LocalBusiness` and `EnergyBusiness` JSON-LD to display exact ratings, location, and services in rich snippets.
*   **Geo-Targeting:** H1 and H2 headers must include specific region or city keywords (e.g., "Photovoltaik für Gewerbe in Berlin").
*   **Performance Metrics:** Target Core Web Vitals to achieve >90 score on mobile.

## 3. Media & Assets
*   **Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG):** All icons, charts, and ROI calculations must be SVG or Code-based HTML/CSS. This ensures sharpness on all displays and trivial text translation without modifying image files.
*   **WebP Images:** All photographs must be compressed to WebP format, maintaining high quality but minimizing load times. Max dimensions 1920x1080 for hero images.
