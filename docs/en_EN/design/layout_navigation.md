# 📐 Layout & Navigation

## 1. Grid System & Breakpoints
The layout uses a 12-column responsive grid system to accommodate complex diagrams, technical data, and rich media.

### Breakpoints
*   **Mobile (xs):** `< 640px` — 4 columns, 16px margins, 16px gutters.
*   **Tablet (sm/md):** `640px - 1024px` — 8 columns, 24px margins, 24px gutters.
*   **Desktop (lg):** `1024px - 1440px` — 12 columns, 32px margins, 24px gutters.
*   **Ultrawide (xl):** `> 1440px` — Max-width container of `1440px` centered on screen.

## 2. Header Structure
The header is sticky to ensure quick navigation to contact options and core business lines.

*   **Top Bar (Dark - Navy-900):**
    *   *Left:* Support Phone Number & Email.
    *   *Right:* "Kundenportal" (Customer Portal) Login Link.
*   **Main Navigation (White):**
    *   *Brand:* Company Logo (Left).
    *   *Links (Center):* Lösungen (Solutions) | Referenzen (Projects) | Unternehmen (About Us).
    *   *Action (Right):* Primary CTA Button "Projekt anfragen" (Request Project).
    *   *Mobile:* Hamburger icon opening a full-screen or off-canvas overlay menu.

## 3. Footer Structure
Structured to provide trust and meet strict German legal requirements.

*   **Top Section (Grid):**
    *   *Column 1:* Logo, Short Description, Contact Info.
    *   *Column 2:* Quick Links (For Home, For Business, For Subcontractors).
    *   *Column 3:* Resources (FAQ, Downloads/Datasheets).
    *   *Column 4:* Newsletter Signup or Social Links.
*   **Trust Block:** Row of certification logos (TÜV, VDE, ISO) on a muted gray background (`#F3F4F6`).
*   **Bottom Bar (Legal):** Copyright © Year, Impressum, Datenschutz, AGB.
