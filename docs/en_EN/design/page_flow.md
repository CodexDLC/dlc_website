# 🗺️ Page Flow & Logic

## 1. HOME PAGE (Gateway Architecture)

### Block 1: Hero Section (Trust & Scale)
Must immediately convey scale and an engineering approach. No cheap stock photos.
*   **Visual:** Widescreen background video or dark photo (overlay `rgba(10,37,64,0.6)`). Shots from a drone showing a large industrial roof with panels or a 3D CAD design process.
*   **Content:**
    *   *H1:* "Zukunftssichere Energielösungen" (Future-proof Energy Solutions).
    *   *Subheading:* "Engineering, Installation, and Maintenance for Industry and Private Homes."
    *   *CTA Button:* "Projekt anfragen" (Request Project).
*   **Trust Bar:** Logo strip of partners (Viessmann, SMA, Fronius) and certificates (TÜV, DIN, ISO) directly below the Hero.

### Block 2: The Gateway (Business Lines)
The main navigation hub distributing traffic. Three large Bento-style cards.
*   **Card A: Privatkunden (B2C)**
    *   *Visual:* Modern house with solar roof.
    *   *Focus:* "Alles aus einer Hand" (Everything from a single source). Subsidies, fast installation.
    *   *Link:* `b2c.html`
*   **Card B: Gewerbe (B2B)**
    *   *Visual:* Warehouse or office building roof.
    *   *Focus:* Energy cost reduction, ROI, ESG standards.
    *   *Link:* `b2b.html`
*   **Card C: Großprojekte (EPC/Subcontracting)**
    *   *Visual:* Blueprint or massive solar park.
    *   *Focus:* EPC services, reliable partnership, strict deadlines.
    *   *Link:* `subcontracting.html`

### Block 3: Expertise & Engineering
Proving the company consists of engineers, not just installers.
*   **Layout:** Split-screen (Text left, Interactive visual right).
*   **Visual:** "Before / After" slider or "Blueprint to Reality" overlay.
*   **Content:** Description of the exact process: from roof statics calculation to grid connection (*Netzanschluss*).

### Block 4: Referenzen (Social Proof)
*   **Visual:** Map of Germany (or specific state) with pins for completed projects, OR a tight grid of 4-6 high-quality project photos.
*   **Metrics:** Large counter animations: "50+ MW installierte Leistung", "200+ erfolgreiche Projekte".

### Block 5: Final CTA
*   **Content:** "Bereit für die Energiewende?" (Ready for the energy transition?)
*   **Action:** Leads into the Step-by-Step Quiz Form.
